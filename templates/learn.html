What is Cyber Attack?

Cybercrime, or computer-oriented crime, is a crime that involves a computer and a network. The computer may have been used in the commission of a crime, or it may be the target. Cybercrime may threaten a person or a nation's security and financial health.

What is Cyber Security?

Computer security, cyber security or information technology security (IT security) is the protection of computer systems and networks from the theft of or damage to their hardware, software, or electronic data, as well as from the disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.

What should I do to be cyber safe?

  • Set strong password

    A strong password is one which contains at least 6 characters that are a combination of uppercase, lowercase letters, numbers and symbols.

  • Remember to log-off

    It is not recommended that you log-in to your account from any unknown device but in case you do, remember to log-off in order to prevent loss of data.

  • Update your device software

    It is advisable to keep your device's software up-to-date in order to prevent cyber attacks.

  • Prevent phishing attacks

    Whenever you receive any mail that seems to be suspicious or too good to be true, do not click any link attached in that mail. You should report such mails as spam.

  • Prevent loss of data and credentials

    Whenever you use any public device or someone else's device use incognito mode so that the browser won't store any credential or browsing information.

  • Check the URL before entering any sensitive information

    Whenever a page loads which asks for sensitive information like bank account information, email id, password etc. check the URL as the cyber criminals often create fake URLs which are similar to the original ones. Also make sure that URL contains ‘https://.’

  • Get anti-virus any your device.

    Use anti-virus on your device to protect your data from theft and also protect your device from general malware and theft of your identity.

  • Read carefully before you provide permission to any software

    You must have seen the pop-ups which comes when you install an app seeking permissions like that of accessing contacts, storage etc. It is important to read all these information and allowing them only if it is necessary and if the app is from a trusted source and trusted developer.

  • Do not download any file from an unknown source

    Never download apps or pirated resources it is illegal and can lead you into legal troubles. Secondly, it is also a medium through which cyber criminals spread malwares and thus the file which one may download thinking it as an app may be a malware.

  • Make sure that the website you are visiting contains ‘https://.’ in its URL

    Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure (HTTPS) is an extension of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) and is used for secure communication over a computer network. In HTTPS, the communication protocol is encrypted using Transport Layer Security and is thus used on website which includes transfer of sensitive information like banking website, e-commerce website etc.
