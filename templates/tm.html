Trending Misinformation

Fake News- Government changes the duration of quarantine from 14 days to 7 days. #COVID-19

A message is being circulated on social networking sites stating that the Indian Government has changed the duration of quarantine from 14 days to 7 days. Government officials told that no such decision has been taken and asked people not to believe on such messages which are circulated on internet.

Fake News- Government announces cancellation of JEE Mains and NEET 2020

A message is being circulated on social networking sites stating that the JEE and NEET exams have been cancelled as per the order by SC. Governments clarifies on this telling that no such decision has been taken and such messages must not be circulated as this may create confusion amongst the students.

A fake message is being circulated through an international number about the free vounchers giveaway by telecom companies.

Since the last week a message is being sent to many mobile numbers telling that the many telecomm companies are giving free data and call vouchers. It was mentioned that in order to claim the vouchers one has to fill in the required information at a link provided as an attachment. It has been announced by the telecom companies that no such offer is there at the moment and have asked customers to check any of the offers via the official website or mobile app.
